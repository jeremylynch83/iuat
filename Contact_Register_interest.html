Register site interest

IUAT is inviting early professional and centre interest from neurovascular physicians, neurosurgeons, interventional neuroradiologists, MDT leads and research teams.

At this stage, registration is not a formal commitment. It helps the trial team understand feasibility and plan the next phase of development.

Suggested information to include

Please include:

  • name
  • role and specialty
  • institution and country
  • contact email
  • whether your centre offers surgical clipping
  • whether your centre offers contemporary endovascular aneurysm treatment
  • approximate annual volume of unruptured anterior circulation aneurysms
  • whether cases are reviewed in a neurovascular MDT
  • any comments about local equipoise or recruitment feasibility
